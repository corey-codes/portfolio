Deets on the dev

I’m Corey.

Much like a great white shark, I’ve never been good at staying still. I’ve always been on the move. When I wasn’t working, I was learning something new or trying my hand at a new hobby. From baking wedding cakes to building and refinishing furniture or becoming an authorized court transcriptionist, I feel like I’ve done it all.

I guess you could say I’m a bit of a jack of all trades who has now chosen to focus on mastering the trade that I’ve become most passionate about, web development.

What started out as a bit of a side hobby has now grown into a full-blown career and obsession. As a former advertising and marketing sales professional, I’ve spent the majority of my career building and delivering presentations and proposals, working with clients to fulfill their marketing goals and collaborating with teams across organizations to get programs launched and run successfully. Now I’m excited to use these skills to develop fully accessible and responsive sites that meet the needs of clients and engage and excite users.
